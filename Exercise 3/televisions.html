Market Analysis

Television insights

A close look at the Australian TV market — screen technologies, sizes, brands, and the energy efficiency that hides behind the spec sheet.

Q1. Which TV screen technologies are most common?

LCD (LED) is the dominant technology, followed by standard LCD and OLED.

Q2. Which screen sizes appear most often?

The most frequent sizes in the Australian market are 65-inch and 55-inch models.

Q3. Which brands have the largest number of models?

Samsung, LG, and Sony maintain some of the most extensive model lineups.

Q4. Which screen technologies use more active power?

Plasma TVs consume substantially more energy than LCD and LED models.

Q5. How does screen size relate to active power usage?

Power use rises as screen size increases, making larger TVs more expensive to run.

Q6. How do star ratings change as screen size increases?

Smaller TVs tend to have stronger star ratings, while larger screens require careful label comparison.

Q7. Which brands tend to use more average power?

Average power varies by brand mix and model range.
