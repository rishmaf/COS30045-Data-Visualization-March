About this project

Understanding the data, tools, and purpose behind EnergyAU.

Project overview

EnergyAU is a data visualisation website built for COS30045 Data Visualisation. The project explores Australian household appliance energy consumption data with a focus on television sets.

Data & methodology

The television dataset was sourced from the Australian Government energy rating database. Data was grouped and aggregated to compare technology, screen size, brand, power consumption, and star ratings.
