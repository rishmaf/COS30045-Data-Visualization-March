Energy-Saving Tips

Simple changes that make a real difference.

Set Smart Thermostat Targets

Every degree above 24°C in cooling can reduce energy use by 5–10%. Set heating to 20°C or below in winter.

Choose High Star-Rated Appliances

Each additional energy star rating can mean noticeably lower energy use over the appliance’s lifetime.

Shift Load to Off-Peak

Run dishwashers, washing machines and EV chargers overnight or during solar midday to cut costs.
